About   IIC

The Institute Innovation Council at IIIT Nagpur is dedicated to constructing a vibrant entrepreneurial ecosystem within the institution. Our mission revolves around nurturing human potential to contribute to nation-building, fostering entrepreneurship awareness, and cultivating an environment conducive to innovation. We aim to instill a culture of innovation-driven entrepreneurship among faculty and students, guiding them towards setting up enterprises for the development of indigenous products and services. Our goals include raising awareness among every individual at IIIT Nagpur, encouraging active participation from students, faculty, and alumni, organizing idea tanks and contests, exploring the entrepreneur world, visualizing products, and laying out plans for startup ventures.

About   (Udyam) E‑cell

Our Udyam Entrepreneurship Cell thrives as a dynamic hub for young visionaries committed to fostering entrepreneurship, innovation, and creativity. We're dedicated to equipping the next generation of entrepreneurs and trailblazers with essential tools, resources, and pathways for realizing their aspirations. Our mission pulsates with the energy of shaping tomorrow's leaders, guiding them towards success in an ever-evolving landscape. With a vibrant spirit and forward-thinking approach, we cultivate an environment where ideas flourish, collaborations blossom, and dreams transform into reality. Together, we ignite the spark of innovation and drive positive change, propelling our community towards boundless opportunities and growth.

About   E‑Summit

E-Summit, the flagship event of IIIT Nagpur, symbolizes the collective ambition and dedication of E-Cell IIIT Nagpur and the esteemed Innovation Council Club. This annual gathering serves as a beacon of inspiration, igniting the flames of innovation and entrepreneurship among attendees. Through thought-provoking keynote speeches, interactive panel discussions, and hands-on workshops, participants are not only equipped with the tools and knowledge needed to navigate the dynamic landscape of entrepreneurship but are also encouraged to push boundaries, embrace failure as a stepping stone to success, and dream big. Moreover, E-Summit serves as a testament to our commitment to fostering an inclusive and diverse ecosystem where individuals from all backgrounds are empowered to pursue their entrepreneurial aspirations and contribute meaningfully to the advancement of society.
